FAQs

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management and it enables companies to manage business-related processes and all the data associated with them. It doesn't really matter what web framework to choose for developing a CRM system: ASP.NET, Spring, AngularJS, Express, Symfony, Django or Ruby on Rails.

Implementing a ready solution will take you a couple of days, however, if you need CRM that fully meets your business needs, it's worth building custom solution, so that the length of your project customers across various channels, such as email, phone calls, social media, and in-person meetings.

The most popular modules are Sales, Marketing, and Service, however, these can be modified or go under other names, for example: Client Management, Order Management, Invoice Management, Events and Tasks Management, System Dashboard, etc.
